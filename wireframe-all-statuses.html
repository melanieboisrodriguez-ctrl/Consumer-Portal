Consumer Portal Phase 2 - Low Wireframe & Design Guidelines

Purpose of This Document

This document provides early UX and UI alignment for Phase 2 of the Consumer Portal. It includes a set of low‚Äëfidelity wireframes intended to illustrate the general direction of the future interface. These wireframes are not final designs and do not represent the complete user flow but establishes a solid directional foundation to support the design and product teams as they develop high‚Äëfidelity mockups.

The objective is to ensure that all teams share a common understanding of the experience principles, the detailed features and intentions we aim for before moving into detailed UX & UI work.

North Star UX/UI Principles

Here is our North Star experience attributes that set expectations for the UX and UI for the Intelcom | Dragonfly Consumer Portal:

  • Simplicity First
    Clean, streamlined interfaces
    Ultra‚Äëintuitive, effortless user journeys
  • Modern Look & Feel
    Contemporary visual design (typography, colors, animations)
    Use of current patterns or smooth transitions inspired by best in the industry or other industries
  • Delight & Emotion
    Small moments of positive surprise (e.g., animated delivery confirmation)
    Friendly, human tone of voice
    Light personalization (name, preferences, history)
  • Visible Innovation
    Dynamic, interactive tracking tools
    Motion elements illustrating delivery progress
    A clear intent to differentiate from competitors by innovating in both design and experience, raising the bar beyond current market standards
  • Accessibility & Inclusivity
    Experiences designed for all user profiles
    Mobile‚Äëfirst navigation, also optimized for tablet and desktop
  • Trust & Transparency
    Clear feedback at every step (confirmation, tracking, issue resolution)
    Visibility into commitments such as sustainability, safety, and accuracy
  • Brand Consistency
    Strong, unified visual identity (logos, colors, pictograms)
    Tone of voice aligned with key brand values: youthful, dynamic, reliable
